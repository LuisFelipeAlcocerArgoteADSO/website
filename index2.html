Steve Jobs

His name was Steve Paul Jobs. He born was on 2 February 1955 and died on 2 October 2011. He was a great informatic and very intelligent visionary. Founded a big company called Apple.
Jobs and apple co-founder Steve Wozniak met in high school. He was the first creator of computer and a great entrepreneur.
It was characterized by visionary ideas in the field of personal computers, mobile telephony, and digital music. Revolutionized the computer industry on big steps for thirty years.
